SMS Consent and Messaging Information

Effective date: March 2026

Book By Text is operated by Genser Group LLC, located in Maryland, United States. This page explains how customers opt in to receive appointment-related SMS messages through businesses using Book By Text.

How Customers Opt In

Customers opt in to receive SMS messages from businesses using Book By Text by texting the business’s publicly advertised booking number.

The booking number may appear on:

  • the business website
  • booking instructions
  • business signage
  • marketing materials

When a customer sends the first message to the booking number, they initiate the conversation and consent to receive automated SMS messages related to appointment scheduling and customer service.

Example Message Flow

Customer texts:

Hi

System response:

Hi — thanks for texting Bella Salon booking.

This service is powered by Book By Text, the automated appointment assistant for Bella Salon.

Reply:
1 – Book
2 – Reschedule
3 – Cancel
4 – Hours

Reply STOP, END, or UNSUBSCRIBE to opt out.
Reply HELP for assistance.
Message & data rates may apply.
Message frequency varies based on appointment activity.

Opt-Out Instructions

Customers may opt out at any time by replying:

  • STOP
  • END
  • UNSUBSCRIBE

Help

Customers may request assistance by replying:

  • HELP
