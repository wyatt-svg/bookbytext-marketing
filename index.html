SMS Appointment Booking for Local Service Businesses

Book By Text is an automated text-first appointment booking platform. Customers can schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments by texting a participating business booking number — without downloading an app or making a phone call.

Transactional Messaging Only

How it works

Customers opt in by texting a participating business booking number (or by providing their mobile number directly to the business for appointment-related messaging). The system responds with a guided booking menu and sends appointment confirmations and scheduling updates.

SMS Notice Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance. Message & data rates may apply.
