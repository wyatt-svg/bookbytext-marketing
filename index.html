SMS Appointment Booking for Local Service Businesses

Book By Text is an automated text-first appointment booking platform. Customers can schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments by texting a participating business booking number — without downloading an app or making a phone call.

Transactional Messaging Only

SMS Opt-In & Disclosures

By texting a participating business booking number, users provide express consent to receive automated SMS messages from Book By Text related to appointment scheduling and customer service. Messages are strictly transactional and appointment-related.

Message frequency varies based on appointment activity.

Customers may opt out at any time by replying STOP, END, or UNSUBSCRIBE.

Customers initiate messaging by texting a participating business’s booking number. Book By Text does not send unsolicited SMS messages.

  • Reply STOP, END, or UNSUBSCRIBE to opt out at any time.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Message and data rates may apply.

How it works

Customers start by texting a participating business booking number (or by providing their mobile number directly to the business for appointment-related messaging). The system replies with a guided menu and sends transactional confirmations and scheduling updates.
